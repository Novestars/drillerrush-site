Driller Rush Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 19, 2026

Driller Rush does not require an account and is designed to be played offline.

Data Collection

Driller Rush does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app stores gameplay progress locally on the device. This local save data is used only to support gameplay and is not sent to the developer or to third parties.

Tracking and Advertising

Driller Rush does not include third-party advertising, tracking, or analytics SDKs.

Permissions

Driller Rush does not request access to the camera, microphone, photo library, contacts, precise location, or user accounts.

Children

Driller Rush does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
